## 📖 About

**SparkyBot** is a powerful, all-in-one Discord bot built to handle everything your server needs — from moderation and ticketing to economy, music, giveaways, and logging. With **18+ modules** and **300+ commands**, it replaces a dozen bots with a single, reliable solution.

**Key Features:**
- 🛡️ **Advanced Moderation** – Kick, ban, mute, warn, jail, purge, and lockdown
- 🎟️ **Ticket System** – Complete support tickets with transcripts and staff roles
- 💰 **Economy & Leveling** – Currency, shop, daily rewards, XP, and level roles
- 🎉 **Giveaways** – Role requirements, multiple winners, and rerolls
- 📄 **Applications** – Custom application forms with review flows
- 🔊 **Join-to-Create Voice** – Private voice channels with full control
- 🎭 **Reaction Roles** – Assign roles via reactions with exclusive modes
- 📝 **Logging** – Comprehensive audit logs for 20+ events
- 🛡️ **Anti-Nuke Protection** – Detect and stop malicious activity (22+ events)
- 🎵 **Music System** – Full-featured music with YouTube, SoundCloud, and Spotify
- 🌐 **Translation** – 100+ languages with auto-translate support
- ▶️ **YouTube Notifications** – Track channels with keyword filters
- 🔒 **Forum Auto-Lock** – Automatically lock forum posts after a set time
- 📨 **Invite Tracking** – Track who invited whom with fake detection
- 🔐 **Verification** – Image captcha verification with private channels
- 🚨 **Reports & Suggestions** – Member reports, suggestions, and bug reports
- 🛡️ **AutoMod** – Spam, invite, link, caps, bad words, and mention limits
- 🔢 **Counting** – Counting channel with saves, streaks, and leaderboards
- 🔧 **Custom Prefix** – Set server-specific prefixes
- 🖼️ **Embed Builder & Rules** – Rich embeds and dropdown rules panels
- 👋 **Welcome & Goodbye** – Custom messages, embeds, DMs, and auto-roles
